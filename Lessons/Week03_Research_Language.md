### 🗣️ B. SPEAKING PATTERNS

#### Pattern 1: Introducing Research Interests

```
TEMPLATE:
"My research examines [TOPIC]. 
 Specifically, I'm interested in how [A] affects [B] in the context of [SETTING].

 I use [METHOD] to study this.
 Currently, I'm working on [CURRENT PROJECT].

 What I've found so far is [PRELIMINARY FINDING].
 This is important because [SIGNIFICANCE]."
```

**Ví dụ 1 — Educational Psychology:**
> "My research examines **the relationship between academic stress and self-regulated learning**. Specifically, I'm interested in how **chronic stress** affects **university students' ability to manage their study time** in the context of **Vietnamese higher education**.
> 
> I use **a mixed methods approach** — combining surveys with in-depth interviews — to study this. Currently, I'm working on **a longitudinal study tracking 200 students over two semesters**.
> 
> What I've found so far is that **students with higher stress levels tend to use fewer self-regulation strategies**. This is important because **it suggests that stress management interventions could improve academic outcomes**."

**Ví dụ 2 — School Counseling:**
> "My research focuses on **the effectiveness of peer counseling programs in secondary schools**. I'm particularly interested in how **trained peer counselors** can **reduce bullying incidents** in the school setting.
> 
> I use **a quasi-experimental design** with pre-test and post-test measurements. Currently, I'm working on **implementing a peer counseling program in three schools in Ho Chi Minh City**.
> 
> Preliminary findings suggest that **schools with peer counseling programs see a 40% reduction in reported bullying cases**. This matters because **it offers a scalable, cost-effective approach to school safety**."

#### Pattern 2: Describing Methodology

```
"We employed a [QUALITATIVE/QUANTITATIVE/MIXED] approach.
 
 Participants were [DESCRIPTION], totaling [NUMBER].
 
 Data was collected through [METHODS].
 
 We analyzed the data using [ANALYSIS METHOD].
 
 To ensure validity, we [STRATEGY]."
```

#### Pattern 3: Stating Findings

```
"Our findings indicate that [RESULT].
 
 This aligns with / contradicts previous research by [AUTHOR].
 
 Interestingly, we also found that [ADDITIONAL FINDING].
 
 The implications of this are [IMPLICATIONS]."
```

#### Pattern 4: Discussing Limitations

```
"I want to acknowledge some limitations of our study.
 
 First, [LIMITATION 1].
 Second, [LIMITATION 2].
 
 However, we tried to mitigate this by [STRATEGY].
 
 Future research should [SUGGESTION]."
```

### 🎧 C. LISTENING PRACTICE

#### Activity 1: Research Presentation Listening

**Nguồn:** Tìm "education research presentation" trên YouTube (15-20 phút)

```
Pre-listening:
1. Đọc tiêu đề presentation
2. Dự đoán: Presenter sẽ nói về gì?
3. Ghi 3 từ bạn nghĩ sẽ nghe

While-listening — Lần 1 (10 phút):
→ Nghe để hiểu ý chính
→ Câu hỏi: Nghiên cứu về gì? Kết quả chính là gì?

While-listening — Lần 2 (10 phút):
→ Ghi keywords: methodology, findings, implications
→ Viết 5 câu bạn nghe được

Post-listening (10 phút):
→ Tóm tắt presentation trong 5 câu
→ Chọn 1 câu và shadow 5 lần
```

#### Activity 2: Methodology Explanation Listening

**Nguồn:** Tìm "how to explain research methodology" trên YouTube (10-15 phút)

```
Task:
1. Nghe 1 video giải thích methodology
2. Ghi lại các bước người ta mô tả
3. Viết lại methodology của nghiên cứu bạn (hoặc giả định)
4. Practice nói methodology trong 1 phút
```

### 🎤 D. SHADOWING EXERCISE

#### Shadowing Texts — Tuần 3

**Đoạn 1: Research Introduction (40 giây)**
> "Good morning, everyone. Today I'd like to present our research on the impact of mindfulness-based interventions on university students' academic performance and emotional well-being. This study was conducted at three universities in Vietnam over a period of one academic year. We used a mixed methods approach, combining quantitative surveys with qualitative interviews."

**Đoạn 2: Methodology (40 giây)**
> "Let me walk you through our methodology. We recruited 200 undergraduate students, randomly assigned to either an experimental group or a control group. The experimental group participated in an eight-week mindfulness program, while the control group received no intervention. Data was collected at three time points: before the intervention, immediately after, and three months later."

**Đoạn 3: Findings (35 giây)**
> "Our findings are quite encouraging. Students in the experimental group showed a significant improvement in self-reported well-being scores, with a mean increase of 15 percent. Additionally, their GPA improved by an average of 0.3 points compared to the control group. These results suggest that mindfulness interventions can have a meaningful impact on both emotional and academic outcomes."

### 💡 TIPS cho Tuần 3

```
🌟 Mẹo 1: HỌC METHODOLOGY QUA CÂU HỎI
   → "How did you study this?" → "We used..."
   → "Who participated?" → "Participants were..."
   → "How did you analyze data?" → "We analyzed using..."
   → Học câu trả lời = học vocabulary tự nhiên

🌟 Mẹo 2: ĐỌC ABSTRACT TO
   → Đọc 1 abstract mỗi ngày bằng tiếng Anh
   → Đọc to, phát âm rõ ràng
   → Vừa học thuật ngữ + vừa luyện phát âm

🌟 Mẹo 3: NÓI VỀ NGHIÊN CỨU NHƯ KỂ CHUYỆN
   → "There's a problem..." (tình hình)
   → "So I decided to study..." (câu hỏi)
   → "What I found was..." (kết quả)
   → "This means that..." (ý nghĩa)

🌟 Mẹo 4: DÙNG CONVERSATION BRIDGES
   → "Let me explain that in simpler terms..."
   → "To put it another way..."
   → "In essence, what I'm saying is..."
   → Giúp người nghe hiểu tốt hơn

🌟 Mẹo 5: CHUẨN BỊ "BACKUP" SENTENCES
   → Khi bí từ: "I can't find the exact word, but what I mean is..."
   → Khi cần suy nghĩ: "That's a good question. Let me think about that."
   → Luôn có câu "cứu nguy" sẵn sàng
```
