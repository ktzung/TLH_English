### 🗣️ B. SPEAKING PATTERNS

#### Pattern 1: Opening a Counseling Session

```
"Welcome. I'm glad you're here today."
"This is a safe space. Everything we discuss will be kept confidential."
"How are you feeling today?"
"What brings you here today?"
"Take your time. There's no rush."
```

#### Pattern 2: Active Listening

```
PARAPHRASING:
"So what I hear you saying is..."
"It sounds like..."
"If I understand correctly..."

REFLECTING FEELINGS:
"It sounds like you're feeling [emotion]."
"I can sense that this is [emotion] for you."
"You seem [emotion] about this situation."

SUMMARIZING:
"Let me make sure I understand. You're saying that..."
"So far, you've shared [main points]."
"Let me pull together what you've told me..."
```

#### Pattern 3: Empathetic Responding

```
VALIDATING:
"That must be really [emotion] for you."
"It takes courage to share this."
"Your feelings are completely valid."
"I can see this is really important to you."

NORMALIZING:
"It's completely normal to feel that way."
"Many students experience this."
"You're not alone in feeling this."

SUPPORTING:
"I'm here for you."
"Let's work through this together."
"You deserve support."
```

#### Pattern 4: Exploring Solutions

```
"What has helped you cope with similar situations in the past?"
"Let's think about some strategies that might work for you."
"What would a good outcome look like for you?"
"Who can you reach out to for support?"
"What small step could you take this week?"
```

#### Pattern 5: Closing a Session

```
"Thank you for sharing with me today."
"I want you to know that I'm here whenever you need to talk."
"Remember, your feelings are valid."
"Let's schedule a follow-up for [date]."
"In the meantime, [key takeaway or strategy]."
"Take care of yourself."
```

### 🎤 D. SHADOWING EXERCISE

**Đoạn 1: Opening Session (25 giây)**
> "Welcome. I'm really glad you decided to come in today. This is a safe space, and everything we discuss will be kept confidential. There's no judgment here. Take your time, and share only what you're comfortable with. How are you feeling today?"

**Đoạn 2: Empathetic Response (30 giây)**
> "I hear you, and I want you to know that what you're feeling is completely valid. It sounds like you've been carrying a lot on your shoulders. That must be incredibly stressful. I can see this is really important to you, and I appreciate you sharing this with me. You're not alone in this."

**Đoạn 3: Closing Session (25 giây)**
> "Thank you for sharing with me today. I know it wasn't easy, and I really appreciate your courage. Remember, I'm here whenever you need to talk. Let's schedule a follow-up for next week. In the meantime, try the breathing exercise we discussed. Take care of yourself."

### 🔤 E. PRONUNCIATION FOCUS — Counseling Voice

```
NÓI ẤM ÁP, CHẬM, RÕ RÀNG:
"I HEAR you." → Nhấn "hear", giọng nhẹ nhàng
"That MUST be HARD." → Nhấn "must" và "hard", giọng đồng cảm
"Tell me MORE." → Nhấn "more", giọng quan tâm
"You're NOT a-LONE." → Nhấn "not" và "alone", giọng hỗ trợ
"How does that make you FEEL?" → Giọng nhẹ nhàng, chờ đợi
"Your FEEL-ings are VA-lid." → Nhấn "feelings" và "valid"

TỐC ĐỘ:
→ Opening: CHẬM, tạo cảm giác an toàn
→ Listening: TRUNG BÌKH, cho không gian
→ Empathy: CHẬM, nhấn mạnh
→ Closing: Ấm áp, kết thúc nhẹ nhàng

TRÁNH:
→ ❌ Nói nhanh (tạo áp lực)
→ ❌ Giọng phán xét
→ ❌ Ngắt lời
→ ❌ Đưa lời khuyên quá sớm
```

### 🎭 F. ROLE-PLAY

#### Scenario 1: Counseling Session — Academic Stress

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
📍 Văn phòng tham vấn học đường
👥 NHÂN VẬT: Student + Counselor (bạn)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Counselor: "Welcome. I'm glad you're here. This is a safe space, 
            and everything we discuss is confidential. 
            How are you feeling today?"

Student: "Not great, honestly. I've been really stressed about exams."

Counselor: "I hear you. Exam season can be really overwhelming. 
            Tell me more about what's been going on."

Student: "My parents expect me to get perfect grades. 
          I study all night but I still don't feel prepared. 
          Sometimes I can't even sleep."

Counselor: "It sounds like you're carrying a lot of pressure — 
            both from yourself and from your family. 
            That must be incredibly stressful. 
            How does that make you feel when you're studying?"

Student: "Anxious. Sometimes I panic and can't focus at all."

Counselor: "Thank you for sharing that with me. 
            Anxiety in these situations is very common, 
            and you're not alone in feeling this way. 
            Your feelings are completely valid. 
            
            Let me ask — what has helped you cope 
            when you've felt stressed before?"

Student: "I used to play basketball, but I stopped because 
          I don't have time anymore."

Counselor: "I see. Physical activity can be really helpful 
            for managing stress. 
            
            Here's what I'd like to suggest: 
            First, let's work on some breathing exercises 
            you can do before studying. 
            Second, let's think about how to communicate 
            with your parents about realistic expectations. 
            Third, let's find small ways to bring 
            basketball back into your routine. 
            
            How does that sound?"

Student: "That sounds helpful. I'd like to try."

Counselor: "Great. Let's schedule a follow-up for next week. 
            In the meantime, try the breathing exercise 
            we discussed — 5 minutes before each study session. 
            
            Remember, you're not alone in this. 
            I'm here whenever you need to talk. 
            Take care of yourself."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

          I eat lunch alone."

Counselor: "That must be really difficult.

            Let's meet again next week to talk about how it went.

### 💡 TIPS cho Tuần 9

```
🌟 Mẹo 1: LẮNG NGHE TRƯỚC, LỜI KHUYÊN SAU
   → ❌ Học sinh nói → Bạn đưa lời khuyên ngay
   → ✅ Học sinh nói → Bạn paraphrase → Hỏi thêm → Rồi mới gợi ý
   → Người ta cần được NGHE trước

🌟 Mẹo 2: SỬ DỤNG IM LẶNG
   → Sau khi học sinh nói xong → ĐỢI 3-5 giây
   → Im lặng = cho không gian suy nghĩ
   → Đừng vội lấp đầy im lặng

🌟 Mẹo 3: PHẢN ÁNH CẢM XÚC, KHÔNG PHÁN XÉT
   → ❌ "You shouldn't feel that way"
   → ✅ "It sounds like you're feeling [emotion]"
   → ✅ "Your feelings are valid"

🌟 Mẹo 4: HỎI MỞ, KHÔNG HỎI ĐÓNG
   → ❌ "Are you sad?" (Có/Không)
   → ✅ "How are you feeling?" (Mở)
   → ✅ "Tell me more about that" (Mở)

🌟 Mẹo 5: CHĂM SÓC BẢN THÂN
   → Tham vấn = emotionally draining
   → Sau mỗi session → tự decompress
   → Nói chuyện với supervisor nếu cần
```
