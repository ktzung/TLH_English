### Ví dụ Presentation hoàn chỉnh

**Slide 1 — Title:**
> "Good morning, everyone. Thank you for being here. Today I'll be presenting our research on **the impact of mindfulness-based interventions on university students' well-being and academic performance**. My co-authors are Dr. Nguyen Van An and Dr. Tran Thi Bich from Vietnam National University."

**Slide 2 — Background:**
> "Let me start with some background. University students worldwide face increasing levels of stress and anxiety. In Vietnam, recent surveys show that over 40% of university students report significant psychological distress. However, most universities focus primarily on academic support, with limited attention to students' emotional well-being. This gap is concerning because research consistently shows that mental health directly affects academic performance."

**Slide 3 — Research Questions:**
> "Our study addresses two main questions. First, does an eight-week mindfulness program improve university students' self-reported well-being? Second, does this improvement translate into better academic performance, as measured by GPA?"

**Slide 4 — Methodology:**
> "We employed a quasi-experimental design with pre-test and post-test measurements. Participants were 200 undergraduate students from three universities in Hanoi, randomly assigned to either an experimental group or a control group. The experimental group participated in weekly 90-minute mindfulness sessions over eight weeks. Data was collected using the Warwick-Edinburgh Mental Well-being Scale, and we also obtained semester GPA data from university records. We analyzed the data using paired t-tests and regression analysis."

**Slides 5-6 — Findings:**
> "Let me walk you through our key findings. As you can see from this chart, students in the experimental group showed a significant improvement in well-being scores — a mean increase of 15 percent compared to only 3 percent in the control group. Additionally, the experimental group's GPA improved by an average of 0.3 points. Interestingly, the benefits were most pronounced among students who initially reported the highest stress levels."

**Slide 7 — Discussion:**
> "These findings suggest that mindfulness interventions can have a meaningful impact on both emotional and academic outcomes. The implications for practice are significant — universities should consider integrating mindfulness programs into their student support services. However, I want to acknowledge some limitations. Our sample was limited to urban universities, and we relied on self-report measures for well-being."

**Slide 8 — Conclusion:**
> "To sum up, our study provides evidence that mindfulness-based interventions can improve both well-being and academic performance among university students. Future research should examine long-term effects and explore implementation in diverse educational settings. Thank you for your attention. I'll now open the floor for questions."

Audience: "Your sample size seems quite small. 
           How do you ensure generalizability?"

You: "That's a great question, and it's something we considered carefully.
      While our sample of 200 students is moderate, we used stratified 
      sampling to ensure representation across different faculties 
      and year levels. That said, I acknowledge this as a limitation. 
      Our findings are most applicable to urban Vietnamese universities. 
      Future research with larger, more diverse samples would 
      strengthen the generalizability."

Audience: "How do you control for the placebo effect?"

You: "That's an important methodological concern. We addressed this 
      by including an active control group that participated in 
      a study skills workshop of equal duration. This helps us 
      isolate the specific effects of mindfulness practice 
      from the general benefits of group participation. 
      However, I'd be happy to elaborate on our specific 
      control procedures if you're interested."

### 💡 TIPS cho Tuần 5

```
🌟 Mẹo 1: LUYỆN VỚI TIMER
   → Đặt timer 5 phút. Nói. Khi hết giờ → dừng.
   → Học cách phân bổ thời gian cho mỗi phần
   → Quan trọng: KẾT THÚC đúng giờ

🌟 Mẹo 2: CHUẨN BỊ "PANIC PHRASES"
   → Khi quên: "Let me come back to that point."
   → Khi slide lỗi: "While we sort this out, let me explain verbally."
   → Khi bí: "That's an excellent question. I'll need to think about that."

🌟 Mẹo 3: NÓI VỚI CAMERA NHƯ NÓI VỚI NGƯỜI
   → Nhìn vào camera (không phải màn hình)
   → Mỉm cười
   → Di chuyển nhẹ nhàng (không đứng yên cứng)

🌟 Mẹo 4: DÙNG "VERBAL SIGNPOSTS"
   → "First... Second... Third..."
   → "Moving on to..."
   → "Let me now turn to..."
   → Giúp người nghe theo dõi dễ dàng

🌟 Mẹo 5: KẾT THÚC MẠNH MẼ
   → "To sum up, our key message is..."
   → "Thank you for your attention."
   → Dừng lại. Mỉm cười. Chờ câu hỏi.
   → ĐỪNG nói "That's all" (nghe yếu)
```
