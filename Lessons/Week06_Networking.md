### 🗣️ B. SPEAKING PATTERNS

#### Pattern 1: Starting a Conversation

```
AT COFFEE BREAK:
"Hi, I'm [NAME]. I really enjoyed your presentation on [TOPIC]. 
 Would you mind if I asked you a few questions?"

AT POSTER SESSION:
"Your poster caught my eye. The [ASPECT] is very relevant 
 to my own work. Could you tell me more about [DETAIL]?"

AT LUNCH:
"Mind if I join you? I'm [NAME] from [INSTITUTION]. 
 This is my first time at this conference."

AFTER A KEYNOTE:
"That keynote was really thought-provoking. 
 I especially liked the point about [TOPIC]. 
 What did you think?"
```

#### Pattern 2: Exchanging Research Interests

```
"So, what's your research about?"
→ "I study [TOPIC]. Specifically, I look at how [A] affects [B]."

"What are you working on currently?"
→ "Right now, I'm focusing on [PROJECT]. 
    We're using [METHOD] to examine [ASPECT]."

"Have you published on this?"
→ "Yes, I have a paper in [JOURNAL] on [TOPIC]. 
    I can share the link if you're interested."
```

#### Pattern 3: Expressing Interest in Collaboration

```
"I think there might be interesting overlap between our research."
"Would you be open to discussing a potential collaboration?"
"I'd love to explore how we might work together on this."
"Do you have any ongoing projects that might benefit from [YOUR SKILL]?"
"Perhaps we could co-organize a symposium on [TOPIC]."
```

#### Pattern 4: Ending Conversations Gracefully

```
"It's been wonderful talking with you. Let me give you my card."
"I don't want to take up too much of your time. 
 Could I get your email? I'll follow up about [TOPIC]."
"Thank you so much for sharing your insights. 
 I'll definitely read your paper on [TOPIC]."
"I've really enjoyed our conversation. 
 Let's stay in touch — I'll send you an email this week."
```

#### Scenario 1: Conference Coffee Break Networking

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

### 💡 TIPS cho Tuần 6

```
🌟 Mẹo 1: MANG THEO CARD VISIT
   → Hoặc chuẩn bị QR code LinkedIn
   → Trao card = chuyên nghiệp
   → Viết ghi chú nhỏ sau card: "Hội nghị X, nói về Y"

🌟 Mẹo 2: HỎI TRƯỚC, NÓI SAU
   → "What brings you to this conference?"
   → "What did you think of the keynote?"
   → Người ta thích nói về bản thân → bạn nghe → tạo connection

🌟 Mẹo 3: THEO DÕI TRONG 48 GIỜ
   → Gửi email follow-up trong 48 giờ
   → Nhắc lại chi tiết cụ thể từ cuộc trò chuyện
   → Đề xuất bước tiếp theo cụ thể

🌟 Mẹo 4: THỰC HÀNH SMALL TALK MỖI NGÀY
   → Nói chuyện với người lạ 1 lần/ngày
   → Bắt đầu bằng: "Nice weather today" / "Great event"
   → Dần dần tăng độ phức tạp

🌟 Mẹo 5: ĐỪNG NGẠI "BỊ TỪ CHỐI"
   → Không phải ai cũng muốn nói chuyện → bình thường
   → Luôn lịch sự: "Enjoy the conference!"
   → Chuyển sang người khác → tiếp tục networking
```
